Our Story

Welcome to La Reine de Saba, where we bring the vibrant flavors and rich traditions of Ethiopia to Paris. Our journey began with a passion for sharing our culture through food, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere for all our guests. We believe that every dish tells a story, and we invite you to experience the authentic taste of Ethiopia, from our spicy Doro Wat to our comforting vegetarian platters, all served with traditional injera bread.

At La Reine de Saba, we source the freshest ingredients and use traditional cooking methods passed down through generations. Our commitment to quality and authenticity ensures a dining experience that is both memorable and delicious. Come and join us to discover a world of culinary delights and cultural richness.
